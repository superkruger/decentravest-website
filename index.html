Features

Whether you are a trader, or an investor, this is the platform for you.

Decentralised

All transactions between traders and investors are performed by a smart-contract on the ethereum blockchain

Peer to peer

Investors pick from the best traders based on their ratings and performance.

Transparent

The smart contract and all transaction data is completely open and auditable.

Safe

Other than trading losses your investment is completely safe, and exit-scam proof.
Investor funds are stored in a Multisig Wallet. Because both the investor and the trader needs to approve every transaction, there is zero chance of your funds being stolen. Your keys, your funds.

Frequently Asked Questions

Questions? Here are some common ones.
If your's isn't covered here, please contact us through our social media channels, listed below.

  • How safe is it to invest?

    As with any speculative investment, the responsibility remains yours. As a platform, we provide the means to link investors and traders, and provide the trading history as well as a rating for traders indicating past performance. But past performance does not guarantee future returns.

  • What about the risk of an exit scam?

    There have been examples of other investment funds stealing their customers funds. We operate differently.
    Firstly, the funds an investor invests, is never actually used to trade with. It stays in escrow in the smart contract, as guarantee against trading losses, and all transaction history is publicly available.
    Secondly, when you invest against a trader, your funds are put in a multisig wallet, requiring both your and the traders' approval for any funds to be moved.
    Thirdly, as an investor you can withdraw your funds at any time.
    In the event of any dispute, the platform can act as a third approver for any transactions.

  • How does withdrawal work?

    Once you've invested funds against a trader, any new trades will result in profits or losses attributed proportionally to your account. Once you decide to withdraw, the trader has to agree that the profit/loss valuation (calculated by the platform) is correct. Once agreed, your original funds plus profits, or minus losses are returned. If the trader fails to respond within 48 hours to a withdrawal request, you are able to force the withdrawal of the original amount minus any losses calculated. This will automatically result in a dispute with the trader being lodged and handled by the platform.

  • How does investment work?

    All you need is a wallet (e.g. Metamask) and funds (ETH, DAI, USDC). No KYC or signup is required. Once connected as an investor, you can pick from the best traders, and invest in them. We do suggest you spread your investment over as many traders as possible to mitigate risk.

  • How does trading work?

    If you sign up as a trader, we'll sync with your trading history on dydx Currently, we only support Isolated Margin trades with ETH, DAI and USDC. You trade with your own funds, using investor funds as a guarantee against losses and a source for trading fees. Your performance and effectiveness as a trader counts toward your rating, potentially attracting new investors. You set your investment limits based on the funds you have available to trade with. Investors then invest in you, until the limit is reached. Any profits made should be paid out once an investor withdraws the investment. Any losses made are recouped once an investor withdraws. It's up to you to reinvest profits into future trades for existing investors, or increase your investment limits to get new investors. Bear in mind that the more investors you have, the less profit they'll make, also affecting your rating. We suggest to keep your investment limits close to the original amounts, plus your 20% share of any profits.
