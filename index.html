Frequently Asked Questions

Questions? Here are some common ones.
If your's isn't covered here, please contact us through our social media channels, listed below.

  • How safe is it to invest?

    As with any speculative investment, the responsibility remains yours. As a platform, we provide the means to link investors and traders, and provide the trading history as well as a rating for traders indicating past performance. But past performance does not guarantee future returns.

  • What about the risk of an exit scam?

    There have been examples of other investment funds stealing their customers funds. We operate differently.
    Firstly, the funds an investor invests, is never actually used to trade with. It stays in escrow in your own personal multi-signature smart contract, as guarantee against trading losses, and all transaction history is publicly available.
    Secondly, when you invest against a trader, both your and the traders' approval are required for any funds to be moved.
    Thirdly, as an investor you can withdraw your funds at any time.
    In the event of any dispute, the platform can act as a third approver for any transactions.

  • How does withdrawal work?

    Once the investor has invested funds against a trader, any new trades will result in profits or losses attributed proportionally to the investor.
    At any stage, either the trader or investor can request to disburse the investment, and the other party has to approve the profit/loss valuation (calculated by the platform).
    Once approved, the original funds plus profits, or minus losses are returned to the investor.
    If the other party fails to respond to a disbursement request, the platform can always force the disbursement of the original amount minus any profits/losses calculated.
    Failure to approve requests might result in loss of privileges on the platform.

  • How does investment work?

    All you need is a wallet (e.g. Metamask) and funds (ETH, DAI, USDC). No KYC or signup is required.
    Once connected as an investor, you can pick from the best traders, and invest in them.
    We do suggest you spread your investment over as many traders as possible to mitigate trading risk.

  • How does trading work?

    If you sign up as a trader, we'll sync with your trading history on dydx Currently, we only support Isolated Margin trades with ETH, DAI and USDC.

    You trade with your own funds, using investor funds as a guarantee against losses and a source for trading fees.
    It's advised to use a DEDICATED wallet for trading with Decentravest, as all profits/losses will be split amongst your investors
    Your performance and effectiveness as a trader counts toward your rating, potentially attracting new investors.

    You set your investment limits based on the funds you have available to trade with.
    Investors then invest in you, until the limit is reached.
    Any profits made should be paid out once an investor withdraws the investment.
    Any losses made are recouped once an investor withdraws.

    It's up to you to reinvest profits into future trades for existing investors, or increase your investment limits to get new investors.
    Bear in mind that the more investors you have, the less profit they'll make, also affecting your rating.
    We suggest to keep your investment limits close to the original amounts, plus your share of any profits.
